About Us

Our Journey

ElevatED is a youth recruitment platform committed to helping young people unlock their potential and prepare for success in today’s dynamic workforce. Our platform specifically focuses on upskilling youth aged 16 to 36 by connecting them with diverse opportunities across various fields of work. We believe that practical experience is key to personal and professional development, and ElevatED is here to bridge the gap between education and employment.

Through our platform, youth can gain hands-on experience in different industries while earning a small income to support their journey. Whether it’s internships, part-time work, freelance projects, or temporary assignments, we provide opportunities that not only build skill sets but also boost confidence and career readiness. We are passionate about creating pathways to success and providing the resources needed for young people to thrive in an ever-changing world.

At ElevatED, we believe that investing in youth is investing in the future, and our mission is to empower them with the tools, experiences, and connections they need to achieve their goals.
